About

Hi, I'm Gwyn. I've spent the last 20 years working in DevOps and Linux infrastructure, from classic data center deployments to large-scale cloud architectures, and everything in between.

Infrastructure Design and Development

I help small businesses by designing and building reliable, well-documented infrastructure. This can be cloud infrastructure, classic data centre infrastructure, or a linux box sitting in a closet. I have designed and deployed hundreds of deployments targetting Kubernetes, and can design and implement the entire software build and deployment lifecycle using your Continuous Integration server and version control tooling. And if you need someone to set up a build server for your teams first, I can do that too.

I do have opinions about gitops workflows with a strong focus on state management being the best solution to infrastructure drift and instability, but I also understand that sometimes you just need your systems to start working so you can keep building your business. If you're interested in a pragmatic engineer solving your business needs while building reproducible systems, please contact me and let's talk.

Teaching and Mentorship

I also work with individuals who want a to learn with a teacher. One on one mentorship, focused on your needs, helps you to learn more quickly. I have 20 years of experience writing command line utilities, shell scripts, and understanding of how linux works under the hood. I teach maintainable shell scripting and testing techniques, and writing more complex software in Go.

I have taught dozens of engineers how to write and test Infrastructure as Code, helping them move from knowing nothing about this approach to having a deployed virtual machine in a couple of hours. I have thousands of hours of experience with cloud providers like AWS, DigitalOcean, or Azure, and teach infrastructure development and design. If you're not sure how the oauth spec matches your cloud provider implementation, or you really want to know how to build and deploy agentic workflows inside the data center, I'm here to help you to learn.

I also design and build modern Kubernetes gitops workflows, and teach how to write custom operators and other tools that natively integrate with Kubernetes. Ever wanted to figure out how the sausage gets made? I can teach you this and more.

I work flexibly with engineers in any timezone. Take the next step to improving your career and learning cool stuff along the way by reaching out by my contact form or booking time on my calendar. I'm looking forwards to working together.
